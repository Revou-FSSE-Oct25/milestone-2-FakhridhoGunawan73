HOW TO PLAY?

  1. Start the Game: Click the "Play Game" button to activate the clicker and start the countdown timer.
  2. Race Against Time: You have exactly 10 seconds to score as many points as possible.
  3. Click Rapidly: Smash the "CLICK ME!" button as fast as your fingers can move before the time runs out.
  4. Final Result: Once the timer hits 0s, the button will lock, and your total score will be displayed on the final board.
  5. Challenge Yourself: Click "Reset" to clear your score and try to beat your personal best in the next round!
