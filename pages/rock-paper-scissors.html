HOW TO PLAY?

  1. Select Your Weapon: Choose between Rock ✊, Paper ✋, or Scissors ✌️.
  2. The Rules: Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, and Paper beats Rock.
  3. Battle the AI: The computer will make its choice at the same time.
  4. Score Points: Win a round to increase your score and score 3 to win the match. Try to outsmart the computer!
  5. Play Again: Use the "Play Again" button to reset the arena and start a new match.
